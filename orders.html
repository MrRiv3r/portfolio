Orders

To place an order please email me with the image ID, which can be obtained by clicking the image you would like a print of, it will appear at the bottom. Include in the email the rough size you would like, which I will do my best to accommodate. Also include your address so I can calculate shipping costs and method. Lastly, prints can be done on canvas, poster paper, and a variety of objects and clothing, so include what medium you would like the print to be on. I will get back to you with the size I can print the image at, a preview of the print, the cost of printing and shipping, and the best estimate on how long it will take to be delivered. All payments are done through e-transfer and printing is done by a third party.
